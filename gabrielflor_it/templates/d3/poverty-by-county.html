A half-decade of increasing poverty

In the last 5 years the average poverty rate in the United States has risen by 2%. According to the U.S. Census Bureau, in 2010 46.2 million people lived in poverty - 5 million under the age of 5. The following map displays the percentage of people living in poverty by county for 2005-2010. Click on a county and use the arrow buttons (or left/right keys on your keyboard) to examine the county's poverty percentage change.
